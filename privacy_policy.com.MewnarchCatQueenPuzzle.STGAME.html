We do not collect data that personally identifies you. The Game itself stores your progress only on your device. However, the Game shows ads through a third-party advertising service, and that service may collect certain device and usage information as described below.

Data type Collected by Purpose
Game progress & settings (current level, stars, coins, audio preferences) The Game (stored locally on your device only) Save your progress so you can continue where you left off
Advertising identifier (e.g. Google Advertising ID / Apple IDFA), approximate IP-based location, and ad interaction/performance data Our advertising partner (Unity LevelPlay / ironSource and its mediated ad networks) Serve and measure ads, limit repetition, and prevent ad fraud

3. Advertising

The Game is free to play and supported by ads (banner, interstitial, and optional rewarded video ads). We use Unity LevelPlay (ironSource) as our ad mediation provider. LevelPlay may work with additional third-party ad networks to fill ad space. These partners may use device identifiers and similar technologies to show ads, including ads that may be tailored to your interests.

You can control or reset ad personalization at the operating-system level:

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → you can reset or delete your advertising ID and opt out of personalized ads.
  • iOS: Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking, and Settings → Privacy & Security → Apple Advertising.

For details on how our ad partner handles data, see the Unity / ironSource Privacy Policy.
